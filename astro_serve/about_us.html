Hosting and Server Technology

AstroServe is built using a modern and scalable architecture. Here's a glimpse into the technology that powers our platform:

  • Backend: The server-side is primarily developed using Microservices, leveraging all its advantages to achieve high performance and data transfer.
  • Data Storage: Uploaded data is stored on our secure, cloud-based servers, ensuring reliability and accessibility.
  • Scalability: The platform is designed to scale horizontally, allowing us to handle a growing number of users and data without compromising performance.
  • Security: We employ industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.
